About Us


We are the theatre dept. for Madison Academic in Jackson, Tn. This is our about us page, which includes mr. young, our current teacher and ms. fly, our lovable retired teacher.

Mr Young

Mr. Young is the new theatre teacher for the 18/19 school year. Mr. Young grew up in Oklahoma and then moved to Mississippi and graduated highschool there. He then went to college at Union University here in Jackson Tn. His first theatre job was at JCM for a year. He then went to teach at Northside for 14 years. Mr. Young claims that his favorite part of theatre is the "live storytelling" and seeing eveyrthing come to life.


Ms. Fly

Ms. Fly was the theatre teacher for Madison Academic for many, many, years. She grew up in Gallatin, Tn and then moved here to go to college at Lambuth and stayed! She startted teaching at USJ for 6 years and then left the theatre scene for 15 years before returning back in 1996, just to come to Madison. Her favorite part of teaching is the confidence that grows in her students, behind or on stage. She also started acting in 1996 also and continued for many years with her favorite movie being "The Last Exorcism." She also was on the board of directors for the theatre association and theatre advisory panel for Tn Arts Commisson.
