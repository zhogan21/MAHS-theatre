About Us


We are the theatre dept. for Madison Academic in Jackson, Tn. This is our about us page, which includes mr. young, our current teacher and ms. fly, our lovable retired teacher.

Mr. Young is the new theatre teacher for the 18/19 school year. Mr. Young grew up in Oklahoma and then moved to Mississippi and graduated highschool there. He then went to college at Union University here in Jackson Tn. His first theatre joob was at JCM for a year then went back to one of his old jobs for a while. He then went to work at northside then proceeded to work there for 14 years up until now. Mr. Young claims that his favorite part of theatre is the "live storytelling" and seeing eveyrthing come to life.


Ms. Fly

Ms Fly was the previous theatre teacher before Mr. Young. She retired after the 2017-18 school year. Aside from her teaching career, Ms. Fly has been in numerous movies, her most notable being The Last Exorcism and a student-made film called Scooters. (need more info)
